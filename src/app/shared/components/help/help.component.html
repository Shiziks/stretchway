The Goal:

5 MINUTES

per stretch, per week

Hold every stretch for 30 seconds, but try to accumulate 5 minutes of holding per stretch per week. If you can not accumulate 5 minutes, than try to accumulate at least 3 minutes, but know that that may slow down the progress.

All You Need To Know:

1. Who is this app for?

If you are a person who would like to increase their flexibility than this app is for you. It does not matter if you are an active person or not, if you regularly exercise or not, you can still use the app.

2. Should I warm up before starting the stretching?

Absolutely! Warming up is essential. You must warm up if you want to see the progress and be safe. Our mucles are made to do strength moves and stretching moves and we should use the full capabilities of our muscles and not only focus on one or the other. Plus strong muscle is a muscle that will stretch more easily.

3. What is the best way to warmup before starting to stretch?

Do at least 10 minutes of warmup before starting to stretch. You can do a cardio warmup (put your favorite song on and dance like no one is watching), or do strength work and than start to stretch. I personly recomend that you do strength exercises before stretching. So if you are about to stretch your legs, than do some squats, lunges, balances... any exercises that do target your leg muscles. You'll know you are warmed up enough when you start to sweat, or if you are a person who does not sweat than as soon as you start to feel the burn in your muscles.

5. Is 30 seconds hold enough?

Absolutely. For some 30 seconds will be too much, and that is ok, you'll build up to that 30 seconds. And for some 30 will seem too litle, and you can hold it a bit longer, but you will see results even if you hold it for just 30s.

6. How soon should I see progress?

It depends on few factors. First is how flexible you are to begin with. Second your genetics. Third the way you live your life. Fourth your dedication to really do the stretches regularly. Some of you may see it in a month and for some it will take longer. I am doing this for 6 months now and I am still seeing progress.
